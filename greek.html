Online 5 Week "Bootcamp" Classic Greek Course

This project was the development of an online 5 week "Bootcamp Classic Greek" course.  It was a collaborative project that included the main instructor who was in California, the Teaching Assistant who was in the Boulder area, the Instructional Designer/Media Production Manager in our department and me, the Lead Instructional Designer. 

The Media Production Manager worked with the instructor to record videos for the course and then I worked with the instructor and the Media Production Manager to layout the course and build it.  I also trained the instructor and the Teaching Assistant on how to use the learning management system and troubleshoot issues during the semester.

This course has been taught the last two summers and will be taught again this summer.  It is a template course and a new instructor will teach the course this summer and will make minor edits to the existing content.
